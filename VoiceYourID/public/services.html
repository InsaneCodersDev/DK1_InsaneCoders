FEATURES



receipt
Voice Your ID

A detailed explanation of the smart attendance system If you are looking for a smart attendance management system that marks attendance based on voice and location and an application where employees cannot spoof their attendance then this is the perfect solution for it.



record_voice_over
Training Model

This feature lets the user record their voice sample which will be used for training their model and whenever the user will try to mark his/her attendance their voice will be compared with this trained model and thier attedance will be marked accordingly.



settings_voice
Voice Recognition

This feature makes marking employees daily attendance really easy.They only have to open our web app and just say few words or 1-2 sentences for marking their attendance and this will only take 5 secs of their busy schedule.



location_on
Location Tracking

Along with the voice the web app also checks for the employees current location and their attendance is only marked if they are present inside the office premises.So only marking attendance through voice is not considered.



phone_in_talk
IVR

Employees can also mark their attendance through the landline which will be available inside the office premises.Here also the employees are supposed to mark their attendance through voice by calling a predefined number.





desktop_windows
Admin Portal

Admin portal has features like new user registration,monitoring the overall attendance,looking after the leaves and exceptional cases,also has a feature where the admin can view the profile of the employees and also has a record of the daily attendance.



recent_actors
Attendance Record

This feature will help the employees maintain a record of their own attendance on a weekly,monthly and yearly basis.It will also show the dates in red when their attendance was not marked.



question_answer
Report Issues

With this feature the employee can apply for a leave or incase of any medical exceptions, this feature lets the employee mark their attendance manually only if they submit proper documents regarding the same.



devices
Cross Platform Compatible

Our system being a web app can be opened on any device i.e. laptops/desktops or mobile phones and it is supported in android as well as ios devices.





feedback
Exception

This feature is available at the admin portal where users can apply for exceptions stating their reasons and attaching the proofs for the same.





thumbs_up_down
Grant Record

For the exceptional cases the admin can grant them leave or allow them to mark their attendance manually and the record for the same is shown at the admin side.





insert_chart
Dashboard

This is the dashboard where the admin can see the daily attendance,keep a track of no of exceptional cases,look out for the pending requests,total no of employees and department count.
